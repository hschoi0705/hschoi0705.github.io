About

I received the B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in EECS, Kyungpook National University in 2005, 2009, and 2016, respectively. Currently, I'm a senior researcher in GITC. I'm interested in Pattern Recognition, Machine Learning, Biometrics and Gesture Recognition. Now I am studying autonomous driving using multiple sensors such as RGB, LiDAR, Radar.
